02 : Wearable device

Development of a Wearable Device for Monitoring Hypertension

Hypertension is a prevalent clinical condition arising due to cardiac abnormalities which needs a continuous monitoring. This paper presents the ground work of non-invasive monitoring of hypertension using the ECG acquired from the subject. The developed hardware was used to acquire ECG signal from 20 subjects. In cardiovascular system, the ventricular mass builds up resulting in abnormal increase in amplitude of R peak and S peak which signify the hypertension condition. Using the ECG signal acquired, the abnormal peaks are detected with the window comparator based on the references set for respective peaks indicating hypertension through LED. It is anticipated that with further optimization and proper prototyping of the proposed system, it should offer an easy way for clinical diagnosis of hypertension and monitoring it through the wearable setup which can prevent cardiovascular risks.

TOOLS USED:- ECG Amplifier | Window Comparator | LED Indicator
